Leonardo da Vinci
Invention Date Location Purpose
Flying Machine 1485 Milan, Italy To study flight mechanisms inspired by birds
Armored Tank 1487 Florence, Italy Designed for military defense with rotating cannons
Self-Propelled Cart Late 15th century Italy Early concept of a robotic vehicle

Flying Machine

A machine with bat-like wings designed to achieve human flight, based on bird anatomy.

Armored Tank

Conceived as a mobile fortress with 360-degree cannons, way ahead of its time.

Self-Propelled Cart

Mechanically driven vehicle that could move without being pushed, a precursor to modern cars.

Robotic Knight

An automated humanoid knight capable of sitting, standing, and moving its arms.
